Motivation

Hi, my name is Matthias Vassallo Pulis, and I am a Final Year student currently reading for a Bachelor's of Science in Information Technology degree, focusing on Artificial Intelligence. I am currently working on my Final Year Project (FYP), titled "Using Computer Vision to Analyse Emotions in a Two-Person Interview" under the supervision of Dr Dylan Seychell and co-supervision of Dr Konstantinos Makantasis .

As part of this project, I am doing a survey to assess how different people visualise different facial expressions. In this survey, you are provided a set of images containing the faces of different people. Your task is to select which emotion best suits the facial expression displayed by the person in the image. You have to choose ONLY 1 out of 7 predefined emotion categories (i.e. happy, sad, neutral, angry, fear, surprise, disgust).
